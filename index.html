TASK 1: DIGITAL TECHNIQUES
Watch: Enlarging details and enhancing images

Digital Techniques Learn

Key Techniques

1
Enlarging Details
  • Use Selection tools (Marquee, Lasso)
  • Copy and paste to new layers
  • Resize details for emphasis
2
Noise Filters
  • Go to Filter > Noise > Add Noise
  • Try 5-15% noise amount
  • Check "Monochromatic" option
3
Color Effects
  • Create new color layers
  • Try blending modes (Multiply, Overlay)
  • Adjust opacity (30-70%)
  • Use colors to connect elements

TASK 2: RESEARCH PAGE

Creating Your Research Page

1
Choose Artist
  • Select artist related to your theme
  • Find good quality images of their work
  • Gather quotes or info about their work
2
Select Details
  • Find important details to highlight

3

4
Add Citations
  • Include Harvard referencing
  • Format: Surname, Initial. (Year) Title [Medium]
  • Create balanced layout
  • Make text readable

Referencing Guide Required

Why Cite Sources:
  • Shows academic integrity
  • Gives credit to creators
  • Shows professional standards
Image References

Format: Surname, Initial. (Year) Title [Medium]

Examples:

  • Kusama, Y. (2016) Infinity Mirrors [Installation]
  • Sherman, C. (1981) Untitled #96 [Photograph]
Quote References

Format: "Quote" (Surname, Year, p.X)

Example:

  • "My art originates from hallucinations." (Kusama, 2002, p.23)

For online sources without pages, omit page number.
